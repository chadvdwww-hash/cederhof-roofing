**Positioning:** Cederhof Roofing is the only premium residential roofing specialist in Johannesburg North presented with AluValue-tier visual craft. The market is full of functional-looking, SEO-heavy contractors; none are visually premium, none ship complete technical SEO, none make the buyer feel they are buying craft rather than labour. This is the opening.

1. Meta descriptions on 100% of pages (HMC 0%)
2. Full schema stack on 100% of pages (HMC 36%)

6. NAP lock with zero divergence (HMC has phone mismatch on /gallery-2/)
7. Breadcrumbs with BreadcrumbList schema (HMC none)
8. WebP + lazy loading + Lighthouse 90+ (HMC JPG only)

- WhatsApp-first CTA (beats 60% of competitors)
- "Free quote in 48 hours" response-time commitment

- Honest process walkthrough addressing "will price creep / will they mess my property" objections

**Craft play:**
- AluValue tonal register translated to roofing: restrained palette, golden-hour hero, generous whitespace, asymmetric section variation, craft-led copy
